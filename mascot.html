My Mascot

Who is he?

On my birthday, on my second trip to Germany, I met a very kind person. Her house was heart-warming, full of stuffed animals, teddy bears and little toys.

When she found out it was my birthday, she insisted on gifting me one of them. I was politely refusing, but once the phrase "You need a mascot" was spoken, something made me think about it. In the end I accepted a little teddy bear and started carrying him around, as a mascot.

A couple hours later, passing by a beautiful lake, I took a picture of him posing on a bench and at that moment I got an idea to make something meaningful out of it: He will be travelling the world! On that same day I started his "Around the world" album and he's been accompanying me on my adventures ever since.

Places visited so far...

Germany

He was born in Germany! I find it really interesting that my mascot comes from a place I admire so much. Of course we've both been there and I could show him some of the countryside and the mountains.

Brazil

After having fun in the snow, it was time to meet the tropical sunshine in Brazil. He's been to wonderful beaches in Rio de Janeiro, and recently had the opportunity to join me on a trip to Manaus, in the Amazon rainforest.

Austria

I was seeing snow for the first time in my life when he joined me. And to have the full experience, we - along with some friends - visited an Iglu resort in Austria. That means ice walls, ice bed, ice ice and snow outside!

It was a wonderful memory even though no Kangaroos were seen... Because no Kangaroos in Austria!!

Switzerland

A visit to the wonderful mountains of the Alps was made and he had an amazing time enjoying the scenery. He couldn't go back home without trying some Swiss chocolate, and now he can't wait to go back there.

And a new friend was made!

After sharing his story with some friends in Brazil, one of them found it really cute and, a few weeks later after travelling to the US, brought him a friend.

At this point they are like brothers and have been spending some time together. They cannot wait for the new adventures life will bring!
