This is my Homepage

As a student of Harvard's Computer Science "CS50" course, the assignment for Week 8's module is to create a simple homepage that introduces myself, my favorite hobby or anything else of interest to me.

I'm not completely sure if I got the "simple" correctly... but this is a great opportunity to learn more about HTML, CSS, Java and Web Design in general anyway.

About me

My name is Gustavo, and I’m from Brazil. I recently graduated as an accountant, and in my current role, I work with various systems, developing reports using Business Intelligence tools and Data Analysis. This experience led me to dive deeper into the world of technology, so I began studying Computer Science to broaden my skills.

I have a passion for learning and exploring new ideas, which is why I also enjoy studying languages—I speak four of them! I believe that the ability to discover, explore, and grow is one of life’s most rewarding experiences.

Though I may sound like a bit of a nerd, I balance that with my love for adventure and extreme sports. I’ve already experienced skydiving, paragliding, kiteboarding, diving, and riding bikes—just a few of the activities I’ve been involved in, and the list keeps growing (hopefully, it never stops).
